Strategic Roadmap

From internal MVP → to sellable Product · v1.0

◆ The Approach
We don't compete against ClickUp. We use free ClickUp to manage day-to-day work while building the MVP as the architectural foundation of the invoicing software — the real sellable product. Every line of MVP code (auth, API, entities, Blazor) is reused in the final product for SMBs.
Track A — Management (ClickUp)
Daily work tool. Tasks, hours, collaboration. Set up once, used always.
Track B — Development (Code)
Building the MVP as the technical foundation of the future invoicing product.
